25 July 1999

Good News

SOFAR's legal team just scored a victory with a judge ruling that the County had waived its deliberative process privilege, and that the County would have to turn over to SOFAR the document in question. This document is the Gogek article with Gary Pryor's handwritten notes as he talked with Larry Prior. Not only is this good news in itself, but it provides justification for disclosure of the remaining documents.
