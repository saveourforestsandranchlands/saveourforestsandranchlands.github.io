RALPHS COMMERCIAL PROJECT
The proposed project would put a 58,000 square foot Ralphs supermarket, a 35,500 square foot second major tenant, retail shops, restaurants, and a service station with car wash in the rural, east end of Lakeside. Much of the proposed site is zoned for agriculture, and the project would require a General Plan Amendment. There is much community opposition.
Current Status: The Planning Commission on 9 June 2000 recommended that the Board of Supervisors deny the project.
