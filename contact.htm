To join SOFAR, learn more about our organization and its work, or to make a contribution to help continue our efforts, please e-mail Duncan McFetridge at SOFAR.

SOFAR is a membership-based group. Membership contributions enable us to continue to engage the help of experts from the fields of planning, land use law, and biological sciences, which support our efforts defending the San Diego backcountry. We are actively working on several campaigns that threaten to diminish the integrity of San Diego County. We invite you to join our efforts by sending a generous contribution to the address listed above or by volunteering time to our campaign efforts. Please call, write, or email us today!
